# Linting & formatting

## Linting

1) **Linting** helps prevent common errors during development.

Things like:
- Syntax errors
- missing arguments
- indentations issues

Linters like Flake8 are highly configurable.(so we will have some words about this subject in v3)

2) Nudges the developer towards best practices

## Formatting

**Formatting** is slightly different, it's more about style and it's very helpful in standardization([Remember](/notes/readability.md#2-standardization) )

**Black** is the most widely used formatting tool.

We consider a lot these 2 concepts in v3 of the improvement of the app.
